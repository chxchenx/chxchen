Archive of Our Own

This project is unaffiliated with Archive of Our Own. Archive of Our Own (AO3) is a popular site to upload fan-created content. This is a design of a mobile version of the site.

This design focuses on preserving the minimalistic attributes of the original AO3 website, reorganizing components so the app is easy to navigate whil still paralleling the website, and making fanworks easy to read while still pleasing to the eye.

This was my first project using Adobe XD and helped me familiarize myself with the program. For future projects, I hope to use more minimalistic design and pay closer attention to clickable components.
